Sandwich

Description

A popular meal both sold in stores/restaurants and often made at home.

Ingredient List

  • Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Turkey
  • Mustard
  • Mayo
  • Pickle Slices

Steps

  1. Apply mustard to bread
  2. Apply mayo to bread
  3. Place lettuce on bread
  4. Add turkey
  5. Add pickle slices
